Academic Accomplishments
Academic Accomplishkments

Our accomplishments are evident in many separate areas; in our numerous arts accolades, our award-winning academic programs, through athletic championships, and most recently in our latest CAASPP achievements in which over 74 percent of Chaparral students were considered at or beyond grade-level expectations in all content areas, highest of all WVUSD middle schools!

One of the Best Middle Schools in America
One of the Best Middle Schools in America

These accomplishments go nicely with Chaparral's multiple designations as a California Department of Education Distinguished School, our two California School Board Association Golden Bell Awards, and of course, our US Department of Education Blue Ribbon Schools Award. In 2019, Niche listed Chaparral as one of the Best Middle Schools in America. Each year, we are ranked by multiple sources as one of the top middle schools in the State of California, and indeed, in the entire United States of America.
